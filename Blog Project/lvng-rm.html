All About Glamour: Top 3 Marble Flooring Designs For Your Hall


Gorgeous marble flooring designs for your hall, as timeless as the material itself!


Marble Floor Design With Diamond Motif


Thi pristine white marble flooring looks gorgeous when scattered with a diamond motif. This type of marble flooring design for halls blends in well with most interior design styles.


Marble Flooring With Borders

Here we have a white marble floor design for drawing rooms. It is accentuated with a border in a darker shade of the stone and makes for another classic flooring pattern.


Straight Lay Marble Tiles

Simpler to use and design, straight lay marble tiles are your best bet to include marble flooring in your modern homes.



Angelic Living Room Decor

For those of you who love the high ceilings, a white colour scheme with a touch of gold patches here and there this living room decor is a heaven on earth. A beautiful stone cladded chimney with a fireplace in the middle is perfect for a winter day. White living rooms are gorgeous the way they are, but every living room needs some decor. This angelic living room is decorated with modern tuxedo sofas in grey and a contrasting white high back single seater sofa. A patterned sofa with it adds to the comfort of seating but also to the living room decor. Artefacts are placed on shelves, and a frosty, golden framed marble centre table adds a modern twist to this living room. Let’s not forget the art pieces made from golden wire to form a ball kept on the table add to the richness of this living room’s decor.



An Autumn Based Living Room Decor Idea

If you love to read and love the autumn season then you might want to consider this simple and easy living room decor idea. This large living is filled with rusty oranges and yellow that brings in a feel of fall. The floor mat is a golden yellow that blends in perfectly with the single armchair and standing lamp with an amber shade. Blue sofas with contrasting coloured pillows are always a fun experiment and look unique too. A floor to ceiling bookshelf with ample space for books is a reader’s dream come true and nevertheless adds to this living room’s decor. A textured wall in grey with a painting in black and white looks beautiful while a decorative globe-shaped glass drop light falls with elegance from the ceiling.



A Patterned Abstract Kind Of Living Room

If you say blue, we got some great small living room decorating ideas for you. Have a small living room don’t let its space bring you down. Let us take your blues away with a pop of blue living room decor. This living room has a sweet little armchair in bubblegum blue that matches the cabinet above the TV. This blue is quite rare, and only a few people fail to recognise its gorgeous eye-popping significance. Take a look at the wall, it has got some wacky paintings pinned up. Remember paintings always add beauty and quirk; they are an essential part of living decor. Fun patterned cushions bring the airforce blue couch to life. An abstract floor mat merges with this small living room decor theme without much hustle. If your a fan of artefacts well you have now got space to show them off on floating shelves made in dark wood.



A Living Room Seating With A Sofa And A Rocking Chair

If you don’t have a living room big enough to add a huge swing chair but you still like the idea of adding a fun seating option then this one’s for you. This living room seating arrangement features a simple two seater sofa with a chic rocking chair as an additional seater. These chairs are easily available in different colours, designs and sizes you can choose the one that works the best for you!



Living Room Seating With A Swing Chair

It’s safe to say that we all love swings! And there’s nothing more fun than having one in your living room! Other than adding fun and style to your living room, a swing chair is also a great seating option, especially if you have kids at home or like to entertain. You can throw in some cushions, quilts and a couple of throw pillows to make it even cosier! These swing chairs are readily available both online and offline in different colours and designs to choose from. Pick a design and colour that blends well with the overall seating arrangement of your living room like the sofa colour, wall paint and lightings.
